Who is this game for: 
People who enjoy grinding games such as WoW, Runescape, Path of Exile, and like to have a character in a persistent world. This game is for someone who likes to have a separate life inside a game where there is a sense of progression and building up/strengthening your character. 

Game design:
Initially there will be developer designed maps, but I hope to expand this to procedural map generation using 'chunks' of pre-created land areas or rooms, similar to Diablo3 map generation.
